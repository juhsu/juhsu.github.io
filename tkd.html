Who We Are

Built on tradition.
Focused on you.

Core Taekwondo is a traditional martial arts studio located in the heart of downtown San Mateo. Founded and led by Master Bryn Nguyen — a black belt in five different martial arts styles with over 30 years of experience in Korean martial arts — the school is rooted in the values of Kodokan tradition: discipline, respect, and continuous self-improvement.

Our curriculum is designed to deliver exceptional results through step-by-step instruction. Students of all backgrounds and abilities develop confidence, sharpen focus, reduce stress, and work toward achieving their black belt in Taekwondo.

In-Person Martial Arts

Ages 4 – Adult

Our core program teaches traditional Taekwondo to students of all ages and skill levels. Classes emphasize step-by-step technique, forms, sparring fundamentals, and the philosophy behind the art — all in a safe, welcoming environment.

🏕️

Camp CoreTKD

Summer Program

Our award-winning summer camp (Top 5 in the San Mateo Area, two years running) runs 9:00 am – 3:00 pm with daily Taekwondo instruction, arts & crafts, outdoor activities, and lunch at Central Park. Friday specials include pizza and water balloon games.

🌙

Parents Night Out

Drop-in evenings

A fun, supervised evening program for kids while parents enjoy a night off. Students participate in games, martial arts activities, and more in a structured, engaging setting.

Beyond the Mat

Community & Service

Community Events

Core Taekwondo students perform and participate across the Peninsula, representing the school at events including the San Mateo County Fair, Lion Dance Festival, Summerfest, College of San Mateo Farmers Market, and the San Mateo Wine Walk.

Teen Leadership Committee

Our black belt students take on leadership roles through event planning, class assistance, and community service — including food drives benefiting Second Harvest and candy donations to Operation Gratitude.

Black Belt Community Service

Every black belt designs and implements a community project — from baby item donations to LifeMoves, food bank volunteering, literacy programs at local preschools, and free women's self-defense seminars.

Core TKD Foundation

A 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to equitable access to Taekwondo training. The foundation empowers students by providing a fundraising platform so financial barriers never stand in the way of training.

Recipients of the Presidential Volunteer Service Award — honoring our students' commitment to making a positive impact beyond the studio.
