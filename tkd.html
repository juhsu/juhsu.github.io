Who We Are

Built on tradition.
Focused on you.

Core Taekwondo is a traditional martial arts studio located in downtown San Mateo, California, instructed by Master Bryn Nguyen, who has been practicing traditional Korean martial arts for over 30 years.

Our curriculum has been developed with the goal of delivering exceptional results. Through step-by-step instruction, students of all backgrounds and abilities gain confidence, improve their focus, reduce stress, and become black belts in the Korean martial art of Taekwondo.

Master Bryn Nguyen

Head Instructor & Owner

  • 30+ years of traditional Korean martial arts experience
  • Black Belt, Taekwondo
  • Black Belt, Kuk Sool Won
  • Family-operated school

Beyond the Mat

Community & Service

Community Events

Core Taekwondo students perform and participate across the Peninsula, representing the school at events including the San Mateo County Fair, Lion Dance Festival, Summerfest, College of San Mateo Farmers Market, and the San Mateo Wine Walk.

Teen Leadership Committee

Our black belt students take on leadership roles through event planning, class assistance, and community service — including food drives benefiting Second Harvest and candy donations to Operation Gratitude.

Black Belt Community Service

Every black belt designs and implements a community project — from baby item donations to LifeMoves, food bank volunteering, literacy programs at local preschools, and free women's self-defense seminars.

Core TKD Foundation

A 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to equitable access to Taekwondo training. The foundation empowers students by providing a fundraising platform so financial barriers never stand in the way of training.

Recipients of the Presidential Volunteer Service Award — honoring our students' commitment to making a positive impact beyond the studio.
