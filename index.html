Who We Are

Built on tradition.
Focused on you.

Core Judo is a community dojo in the heart of San Mateo dedicated to teaching the art of judo to teens and adults of all experience levels. Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or sharpening competition skills, our instructors meet you where you are.

Judo — the "gentle way" — builds physical strength, mental discipline, and lifelong confidence. Our classes emphasize proper technique, respect, and a welcoming environment that makes training both challenging and rewarding.

Sister School

Core Judo shares a home with Core Taekwondo, an award-winning martial arts school in San Mateo. Together we offer a full range of martial arts programs under one roof.

Meet the Sensei

Our instructors

Paul C.

Head Instructor

Sensei Paul is a shodan (first-degree black belt) in judo with over 10 years of competitive experience on the mat, training under Cahill's Judo Academy. Dedicated to the holistic development of practitioners, he focuses on teaching the fundamentals of traditional Kodokan judo, emphasizing proper technique, safety, and respect. As head instructor of Core Judo, he has fostered a supportive, family-friendly environment focused on character development and personal growth. Sensei Paul is passionate about tailoring training to suit everyone from first-time students to seasoned competitors, ensuring every student feels confident and welcomed.

  • Black Belt, Judo
    • Certified USA Judo Shodan

Justin H.

Instructor

Sensei Justin received his judo black belt from the legendary Willy Cahill, one of the most decorated judo coaches in American history. He brings over 30 years of martial arts experience across judo, Brazilian jiu-jitsu, wrestling, and taekwondo, and is known for his ability to break down complex technique and make it accessible at every level. A newaza specialist, he remains an active competitor on the Masters and Veterans circuits in BJJ and judo respectively.

  • Black Belt, Judo
    • Certified USA Judo Shodan
  • Purple Belt, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
    • Multi-time IBJJF medalist (incl. IBJJF Worlds, M1 Purple)
  • Black Belt, Taekwondo
