Who We Are

Built on tradition.
Focused on you.

Core Judo is a community dojo in the heart of San Mateo dedicated to teaching the art of judo to teens and adults of all experience levels. Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or sharpening competition skills, our instructors meet you where you are.

Judo — the "gentle way" — builds physical strength, mental discipline, and lifelong confidence. Our classes emphasize proper technique, respect, and a welcoming environment that makes training both challenging and rewarding.

Sister School

Core Judo shares a home with Core Taekwondo, a two-time award-winning martial arts school in San Mateo. Together we offer a full range of martial arts programs under one roof.

Teens

Ages 13 – 17

A structured program designed for teens that builds physical fitness, self-defense skills, and mental focus. Students learn throws, groundwork, and competition fundamentals in a supportive group setting.

🏅

Adults

Ages 18 & up

Open to all adult skill levels — beginner through advanced. Classes cover traditional judo techniques alongside conditioning, sparring, and the philosophy behind the sport. A great full-body workout and community.
